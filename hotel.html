Standard Rooms

For your comfort, The Country Inn's Standard Rooms feature top of the line extra long mattresses, individual thermostat controlled heating and air conditioning, satellite TV, complimentary highspeed wireless internet and work desk.

Delux Business Rooms

The Country Inn's deluxe rooms are larger and roomier, for added comfort for the busy executive. For your comfort, our deluxe rooms feature top of the line Simmons Undisturb pillow top matresses, custom made maple furniture, a large work desk.

King Suite

For your comfort, suites feature king size beds, custom made cherry furniture, marble washroom floors, 40" LCD and complimentary highspeed wireless internet. Your comfort is our top priority.

Master Suite

For a special evening try our Executive Master Suite. Our suites feature a 32 jet hot tub, fireplace, seating area, separate washroom, private balcony plus all of the amenities you would expect of a suite.
